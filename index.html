Mateusz Klimaszewski

PhD Student @ WUT, currently @ UoE

I am a fifth-year PhD student at the Institute of Computer Science at the Warsaw University of Technology. I am working under the supervision of Prof. T. Gambin and P. Andruszkiewicz on Neural Machine Translation, Modular Deep Learning and Domain Adaptation.

Recently, I was a Research Intern at Booking.com working with the NMT team. I have also done internships at Google Zurich and Amazon Luxembourg. Additionally, I did two research visits at The University of Edinburgh working in the StatMT group under the supervision of A. Birch.
